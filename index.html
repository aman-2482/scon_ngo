About Us

SCON (Society for Conservation of Nature) is a dedicated non-profit organization working towards the conservation of vultures and wildlife. Our team conducts awareness campaigns, rescue operations, and educational programs to highlight the importance of protecting these majestic scavengers, which are critical to our ecosystem.
