SCON - Society for Conservation of Nature

Dedicated to the conservation of vultures and the preservation of our ecosystems.

About Us

SCON (Society for Conservation of Nature) is a dedicated non-profit organization working towards the conservation of vultures and wildlife. We understand the crucial role vultures play in maintaining a balanced ecosystem by preventing the spread of diseases and reducing environmental contamination.

Through our efforts, we are committed to ensuring a healthy, sustainable future for these important birds and their habitats.

Our Mission

Our mission at SCON is to promote the conservation of vultures and other endangered species across the globe. By focusing on awareness campaigns, community outreach programs, and direct conservation efforts, we aim to safeguard the future of these vital creatures.

Key Objectives

  • Raise awareness about the vital role of vultures and their conservation needs.
  • Provide education to local communities about the benefits of wildlife conservation.
  • Initiate rescue operations for injured or sick vultures and rehabilitate them for release back into the wild.
  • Collaborate with governments and organizations to protect the habitats of endangered species.
  • Conduct research on vulture populations, migration patterns, and threats they face.
  • Support local initiatives to prevent illegal poaching and poisoning of vultures.

Our Projects

Some of our key projects include:

  • Vulture Awareness Campaigns: Spreading awareness about the importance of vultures to communities and policymakers.
  • Vulture Rescue Programs: Coordinating with local wildlife hospitals to rescue and rehabilitate vultures.
  • Habitat Protection Initiatives: Working with conservation agencies to protect critical vulture habitats.
  • Educational Workshops: Educating schools and local groups on the importance of preserving vultures and biodiversity.
